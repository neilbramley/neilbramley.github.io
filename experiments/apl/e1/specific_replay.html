Instructions

  1. Select the participant and trial you would like to see below the window.
  2. Click "Load" then "Start" to play a trial.
  3. Pause, Resume and Stop and (Re)start using the buttons.
  4. Select a different settings and click "Load" again to view different participants and trials.
  5. Refresh the page if it breaks, If it is still broken email nrbramley@gmail.com :).
  6. The window at the top right gives details of the chosen trial (world index and true relationship and mass).
